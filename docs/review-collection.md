# Review Collection Guide

Matcha Scout reviews are short, taste-focused notes from people who have actually tried a drink. They help turn a verified menu item into a useful recommendation target.

Review link: https://matcha-scout.vercel.app/review

## Why Reviews Matter

The app can list verified drinks from admin curation, but recommendations get better only when real people describe what those drinks taste like. A good review gives the system signal about matcha strength, sweetness, creaminess, earthiness, and bitterness.

Yelp ratings, Google reviews, copied posts, and cafe popularity do not count toward Matcha Scout confidence. Confidence is based only on Matcha Scout reviews submitted for that exact drink.

## Confidence Levels

- `unrated`: 0 Matcha Scout reviews
- `low`: 1 Matcha Scout review
- `medium`: 2-4 Matcha Scout reviews
- `high`: 5+ Matcha Scout reviews

## What To Write

Write like you are telling a friend what the drink tasted like. Mention the parts that stood out:

- matcha strength: bold, grassy, mild, tea-forward
- sweetness: unsweetened, lightly sweet, very sweet
- creaminess: watery, milky, creamy, thick
- earthiness: earthy, vegetal, mellow, floral
- bitterness: smooth, slight bitterness, bitter finish
- milk or flavor add-ins if they changed the taste

Good review examples:

- "Strong matcha flavor, lightly sweet, creamy oat milk, slight bitterness."
- "Mostly sweet strawberry flavor, not very earthy, very creamy."
- "Smooth and mellow, more milk than matcha, barely bitter."
- "Earthy and tea-forward with almond milk, not too sweet."

## What Not To Submit

Do not submit:

- fake reviews
- reviews for drinks you have not tried
- copied Yelp, Google, Beli, TikTok, Instagram, or blog text
- general cafe reviews that do not describe the drink
- comments about parking, service, ambience, or wait time unless they also include taste notes

## How To Help

1. Go to https://matcha-scout.vercel.app/review
2. Pick a drink you have tried.
3. Open the drink page.
4. Submit a taste-focused review in your own words.

## Current Launch Target

The first launch target is to get each verified drink from 0 reviews to at least 1 real review. After that, the next target is to push the most popular or most-requested drinks to 2-4 reviews so they reach medium confidence.

Current production state:

- 305 cafes
- 24 verified drinks
- 24 review targets
- San Diego has the first verified drink batch
- Orange County cafes exist, but the Orange County verified drink batch has not been added yet
