## LinkedIn-Friendly Post

I built Matcha Scout, a small matcha discovery app for San Diego and Orange County.

The current launch goal is review collection: verified drinks are live, but they need real taste reviews before recommendations become useful. The review flow asks people to describe what they tasted - matcha strength, sweetness, creaminess, earthiness, and bitterness - then the app turns those notes into taste profiles and confidence levels.

If you are in SD/OC and have actually tried one of the listed drinks, I would love a quick review:
https://matcha-scout.vercel.app/review

Tech stack: Next.js, FastAPI, DynamoDB, AWS Lambda/API Gateway, and Vercel.

## 30-Second Spoken Pitch

I built Matcha Scout because matcha recommendations are usually too generic. Instead of just using cafe ratings, it asks what a drink actually tastes like - how strong, sweet, creamy, earthy, or bitter it is. The first verified San Diego drinks are live, and now I need real taste reviews from people who have actually tried them. The goal is to get every verified drink from zero reviews to at least one, then get the most popular ones to a few reviews so recommendations become more reliable.
